Research

Project Sidewalk
May 2018 - present
Makeability Lab at University of Washington

Characterizes street-level accessibility of cities using a combination of crowd-sourcing techniques, computer vision, and machine learning. I primarily work on the online tool that allows users to physically audit cities in Washington DC and label accessiblity features using JavaScript/HTML/CSS and the Google Maps API. I have also helped configure the project envioronment with Docker to deploy to production.
